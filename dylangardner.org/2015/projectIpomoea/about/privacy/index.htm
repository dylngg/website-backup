Privacy?

Lets be honest. I don't track you, you don't track me... Cool? If you need some reassurance, here's why I don't want to track you.

  1. It's more fun dreaming about who visits my site than actually knowing.
  2. I respect your wishes to not be known.
  3. There is no point for me to know.
  4. With Goverments, Companies and Hackers tracking you, Privacy has become a luxury. I want to provide you with this when you visit.
  5. I don't want google tracking you even if you aren't on their site. (Google Ads)
  6. I really don't want your information or want to sell it. (You're not useless, I just don't believe in companies using your data for $)
  7. I hate ads with a burning passion so, why would I help people give ads to you and me?

If you don't understand all this, just search "does google track me" or want to know about governments tracking you go here.
